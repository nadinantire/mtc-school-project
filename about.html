Uko muhanga technical center yavutse

Muhanga Technical Center (MTC) ni ishuri ryigenga ryigisha imyuga n'ubumenyingiro riherereye mu ntara y'amajyepfo, akarere ka muhanga, umurenge wa nyamabuye, akagali ka gahogo umudugudu wa nyarucyamo.

Iri shuri ryatangiranye n'umwaka w'amashuri wa 2012 ritangira ari VTC rifite amashami atatu: ubwubatsi, amashanyarazi n'ubukanishi bw'imodoka.

EMMANUEL HAVUGIMANA

Emmanuel Havugimana

Umuyobozi w'Ishuri Muhanga Technical Center

Emmanuel Havugimana ni umwe mu bashinze ishuri rya Muhanga Technical Center mu mwaka wa 2012. Afite intego yo guteza imbere urubyiruko rw'u Rwanda binyuze mu myigishirize y'imyuga n'ubumenyingiro ndetse no kubafasha kwihangira imirimo.

Yiyemeje guhanga ishuri rizaba icyitegererezo mu Karere ka Muhanga ndetse no mu Rwanda rwose, ritanga uburezi bufite ireme kandi buhuye n'ibikenewe ku isoko ry'umurimo.
